About Deck Giant

A Business Built On Extreme Competence.

Deck Giant is owned and run by two Colorado-based builders who've spent their careers in construction, design, and project management — not a franchise, not a subcontractor network. When you call, you're talking to one of us.

Gary and Justin are longtime friends who realized their skill sets were built to complement each other — hands-on construction experience paired with business and finance know-how. That combination is what lets Deck Giant run as an efficient, well-organized company that never loses sight of what the customer actually needs.

OWNER & PROJECT MANAGER

Gary Tussey

A Colorado native with 20+ years of hands-on construction and carpentry experience, Gary also spent a decade in 3D medical, military, transportation, and aerospace animation — including classified defense contract work — after graduating from the Art Institute of Colorado in 1999. He's an Emmy Award-winning animator and director, fluent in AutoCAD, 3ds Max, and Maya.

That combination of construction know-how and 3D visualization skill means Gary can turn an idea into a detailed drawing or realistic rendering before a single post goes in the ground — helping clients see the space and solve tricky design details early. As Owner and Project Manager, he oversees every project from design through completion.

OWNER & SALES MANAGER

Justin Fitzpatrick

Justin brings 15+ years as a business owner and construction professional. He previously owned and ran a construction company specializing in high-end decks, pergolas, and carports before selling it, and served as head project manager for a real estate firm overseeing multi-million-dollar renovation budgets on multifamily and commercial properties.

He holds an Associate of Applied Science in Building Construction Technology and Construction Management from Austin Community College. At Deck Giant, Justin runs sales and business operations, working with clients from the first conversation through project planning. Outside of work he's usually with his wife and daughter, or out woodworking, adventure motorcycling, fly fishing, camping, rock climbing, or exploring the outdoors with his dog.
