Use this as a baseline for planning. Every project is different — size, site conditions, terrain, and material choice all affect the final price. Your written estimate after a free on-site visit will have the real numbers.

We offer free on-site consultations and free preliminary quotes via email for every project — large or small. See our Our Process page for more info.

Combo project? Save 10% on the total cost when you build a deck and pergola — or any two structures — together as one project.

Price match policy. Because we're a small, family-run business with low overhead, we can be very competitive with our pricing. We'll attempt to match prices from legitimate, insured, local competitors on an equal product.
