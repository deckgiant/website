Custom Pavilions, Built for Colorado.

Deck Giant designs and builds custom cedar, gable-roof pavilions for homeowners throughout the south Denver metro and out into the Colorado foothills — built as a true outdoor room, not just shade.

Pavilions are one of Deck Giant's core specialties — a fully roofed, finished structure built for year-round use, not just shade. Whether you want a simple covered sitting area or a full outdoor kitchen and fireplace setup, we design and build it to fit your property and your needs. We thrive in difficult terrain, steep slopes, and complicated projects — no job is too complex.
